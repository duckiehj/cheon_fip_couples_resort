Rooms Welcome Information

Unwind in spacious suites and private cottages designed just for two. Choose from our Lakeside Cabins, offering rustic seclusion and natural charm, or our Lakeside Rooms, each uniquely styled with elegant or cozy touches. Every space blends luxury and nature—featuring lake views, hot tubs, wood-burning fireplaces, and thoughtful details from Old European to Haliburton-inspired design. Every detail is curated to help you relax, reconnect, and make lasting memories.
