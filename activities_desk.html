Activities Welcome Information

Discover a world of adventure and tranquility at Couples Resort. Surrounded by the pristine beauty of Algonquin Park and nestled on the shores of Soyers Lake, our resort offers year-round activities for every kind of couple. Algonquin Park

Algonquin Park Information

Just minutes from Couples Resort, Algonquin Park invites you into one of Canada’s most iconic wilderness destinations. Spanning over 7,600 square kilometers, the park is home to crystal-clear lakes, dense forests, and diverse wildlife—including moose, beavers, and loons. Whether you're hiking scenic trails, paddling through quiet waters, or snowshoeing across winter landscapes, Algonquin offers unforgettable experiences in every season. With its rich history as Ontario’s first provincial park and its breathtaking natural beauty, it’s the perfect place to reconnect with nature—and each other.

Guests receive a FREE Algonquin Park Day Pass, giving access to trails, wildlife viewing, and museums. (Note: Fall weekends may require advance purchase—fully reimbursed during your stay.) For more information, contact the Resort Desk.
